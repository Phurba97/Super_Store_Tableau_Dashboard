**Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard | Tableau
**
https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/phurba.doma.sherpa/viz/TableauAssignmentSolution_17777981278310/Assignment7

An interactive Tableau dashboard built using the Sample Superstore dataset to analyze business performance across Sales, Profit, and Orders. The dashboard provides an executive overview with KPI cards, category and sub-category analysis, geographic insights, customer segment distribution, top-performing products, and sales trends. Users can dynamically switch between metrics and apply Region and Year filters for deeper analysis.

**Features**

Dynamic metric selection (Sales, Profit, Orders) 

KPI cards for Sales, Profit Margin, and Return Rate

Category and Sub-Category performance analysis

Interactive map showing state-wise performance

Customer Segment analysis

Top 5 Products by selected metric

Monthly trend analysis with trend line

Region and Year filters for interactive exploration

**Key Insights
**

Technology is the highest-performing category in terms of sales and profit.

Consumer segment contributes the largest share of overall sales.

California and New York are among the top-performing states.

Some sub-categories, such as Tables and Bookcases, generate lower or negative profits, highlighting areas for improvement.

Sales show a consistent upward trend over time.

A small number of products drive a significant portion of revenue and profit.

Although sales are increasing over time, certain products and categories require margin optimization to improve overall profitability.


**Sales Analysis
**

This dashboard analyzes sales performance across categories, products, customer segments, and states. It includes KPI cards for Total Sales, Profit Margin, and Return Rate, along with visualizations for category-wise sales, state-wise sales, top-performing products, and monthly sales trends. Users can filter data by Region and Year to gain deeper business insights.

**Profit Analysis
**

This dashboard focuses on profitability analysis by visualizing profit across categories, sub-categories, states, customer segments, and products. It helps identify profitable and loss-making areas, highlights top profit-generating products, and tracks profit trends over time. Interactive filters enable detailed analysis by Region and Year, supporting data-driven business decisions.

Orders Analysis

This interactive dashboard provides an overview of order performance across the Superstore dataset. It highlights total orders by category, sub-category, state, customer segment, top-selling products by order volume, and monthly order trends. Interactive filters for Region and Year allow users to explore order patterns and identify high-demand products and locations.
